Category Description
Theory Applies required knowledge including airspace structure, SOPs, LoAs.
Phraseology/Radiotelephony Applies correct phraseology in English and German.
Coordination Performs the required coordination with neighboring stations clearly and effectively. Hands/takes over station correctly. Uses correct vocabulary (e.g. approval request).
Tag management/FPL Handling Keeps flight plan and tag up to date at all times.
Situational awareness Aware of the current and future traffic situation. Takes new information into account. Knows his own limits and limits of the airspace/airport, asks for relief when appropriate.
Problem recognition Recognizes problems early and reacts accordingly.
Traffic planning Looks ahead and plans a secure and efficient traffic flow.
Reaction Reacts in a timely manner, flexible and appropriate to changes in the current traffic situation.
Separation Applies prescribed separation minima at all times (i.e. runway, radar, wake turbulence, separation etc.).
Efficiency Takes pilot's requests into account, handles traffic in an efficient way for himself, the downstream sector and the pilot.
Ability to work under pressure Shows consistent performance regardless of traffic volume. Recovery from mistakes.
Manner and motivation Is open to feedback and makes a realistic assessment of own performance. Deals respectfully with others and is well prepared for the session.
